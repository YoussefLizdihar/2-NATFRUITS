About Organic Food

Organic food are grown without the use of synthetic pesticides, chemical fertilizers, or genetically modified organisms (GMOs). They are cultivated using natural farming practices that prioritize soil health, biodiversity, and environmental sustainability. Unlike conventionally grown fruits, organic produce is subject to strict certification standards to ensure its authenticity and quality.
